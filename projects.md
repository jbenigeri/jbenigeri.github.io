### Video Game Price Analysis

A data science project analyzing Steam video game pricing patterns in the European market (2015-2024). Investigates whether game prices have kept pace with inflation and how COVID-19 affected pricing strategies. Uses HICP (Harmonised Index of Consumer Prices) to compare real vs nominal prices across years.

[View the interactive analysis notebooks ‚Üí](https://jbenigeri.github.io/Video-Game-Price-Analysis/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/jbenigeri/Video-Game-Price-Analysis)

### Hand Gesture Detection

Detects gestures like üëç Thumbs Up, üëé Thumbs Down, ‚úã Raised Hand, and üëè Clapping using hand landmarks + heuristics. Includes a Streamlit data collection dashboard for recording labeled gesture samples, plus ML model training (Random Forest, SVM) to expand detection to more gestures without sacrificing accuracy‚Äîsince pure heuristics get confused by similar hand positions.
